How to Measure

Bust: Around the fullest part, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
Waist: At the natural waistline, typically the narrowest point.
Hip: Around the fullest part of the hips, about 8" below the waist.

Fit Recommendations

Relaxed / ethnic: Size up for draped fits.
Tailored / office: True to size.
Bodycon: Snug fit — consider your preferred ease.

Free Size & Accessories

Sarees, dupattas, and select ethnic pieces marked Free Size are designed to accommodate a wide range of body types through adjustable draping or generous cuts. Accessories marked One Size — including jewellery, scarves, bags, and sunglasses — are universally sized unless otherwise noted on the product page.
