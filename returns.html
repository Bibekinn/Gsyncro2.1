Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love every GSYNCRO piece. If something is not quite right, our returns process is straightforward, transparent, and handled with the same care we put into every order.

Return Window

You may return eligible items within 14 days of delivery for a full refund to your original payment method. For exchanges, we recommend initiating your request within 7 days to ensure your preferred size remains available.

Product Condition

To qualify for a return or exchange, items must be:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and free from perfume, makeup, or damage
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by your order confirmation or receipt

Non-Returnable Items

The following cannot be returned for hygiene and quality reasons:

  • Earrings and intimate apparel (unless faulty)
  • Items marked as final sale or limited edition (where stated at purchase)
  • Customised or altered pieces
  • Gift cards

Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. Depending on your bank or payment provider, funds may take an additional 3–10 business days to appear on your statement.

Exchange Process

To exchange a size or colour, contact us at Contact with your order number. We will guide you through return shipment and reissue of your preferred item. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

Sale items may be exchanged for store credit only unless defective. International return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.
