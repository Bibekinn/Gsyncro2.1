Delivery

Shipping Information

Every GSYNCRO order is prepared with care and dispatched through trusted carriers. We deliver worldwide with transparent timelines and complimentary shipping on qualifying orders.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. During peak seasons or new collection launches, processing may extend to 3 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

Complimentary Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Orders below this threshold are charged a flat rate of $9.99 at checkout.

Domestic Shipping

Within Nepal and select South Asian regions, standard delivery typically arrives within 3–7 business days after dispatch. Express options may be available at checkout where supported.

International Shipping

We ship to the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, the EU, and additional destinations worldwide. International standard delivery generally takes 7–14 business days from dispatch.

Shipping Costs

Standard rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight. Promotional free shipping applies automatically when your subtotal exceeds $50.

Customs duties, import taxes, and local handling fees may apply for international orders. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in GSYNCRO product or shipping prices.

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. Track your parcel anytime on our Track Order page. Allow 24–48 hours for tracking information to activate after you receive your shipping confirmation.

For delivery questions, visit Contact — our team responds within 1–2 business days.
