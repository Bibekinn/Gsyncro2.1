Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025. This policy explains how GSYNCRO collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you shop with us.

Information We Collect

When you place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, order history, and payment-related data processed securely by our payment partners.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, maintain your shopping bag, and understand how visitors use our website. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

Payments & Security

Payment information is handled by PCI-compliant third-party processors (e.g. Stripe). GSYNCRO does not store full card numbers on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfil orders
  • To communicate order updates and customer support responses
  • To send marketing communications where you have opted in
  • To improve our website, products, and services

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@gsyncro.com.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.
